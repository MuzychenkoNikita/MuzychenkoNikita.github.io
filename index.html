About Me

Nikita Muzychenko

Graphics Engineering Student

I'm a graphics engineering student passionate about real-time rendering, computer graphics, and visual computing. My journey began with curiosity about how games and 3D applications create stunning visuals, which led me deep into the world of GPU programming and computational graphics.

Currently pursuing my degree while building rendering engines, exploring advanced shader techniques, and contributing to open-source graphics projects. I believe in the intersection of mathematics, art, and engineering to create experiences that are both technically impressive and visually beautiful.

When I'm not writing shaders or optimizing rendering pipelines, you'll find me exploring photography, studying mathematics, or experimenting with generative art.
